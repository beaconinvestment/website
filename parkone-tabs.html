Location

Park one is near Al-Mustafa tower in F-10. Located on the 10th Avenue at the foot of Margalla Hills, adjacent to the F-10 commercial block facing the Fatima Jinnah Park, Park One provides the best of city’s attractions and facilities.

The architecture of Park one is an assimilation of twenty one floors divided over shopping arena and residential complex. Concourse to level two will feature a Shopping Mall, whereas level three to level 17 will house luxurious apartments of different types.
